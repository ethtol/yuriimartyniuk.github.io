// About

Engineer behind the platform.

Cloud-native by trade, security-minded by habit.

I'm a Senior DevOps Engineer based in Lviv, Ukraine, with seven years of experience turning fragile, manually deployed systems into platforms that scale, recover gracefully and audit cleanly.

My day-to-day spans VPC topologies, EKS clusters, CI/CD pipelines, IaC modules and observability stacks. I'm fluent in AWS, comfortable in GCP and Azure, and I've led several end-to-end cross-cloud migrations with zero data loss and minimal downtime.

I also enjoy the harder parts of the job: passing ISO 27001 and SOC 2 audits, designing multi-account governance with Control Tower, hardening Kubernetes baselines, and building disaster-recovery plans that actually get tested.

Outside of project work I write technical articles on Medium, speak at AWS meetups, and mentor engineers on the path from sysadmin to cloud architect.

// Audits & Compliance

Audit-ready infrastructure.

I've helped organizations pass audits — not just prepare for them.

PASSED

ISO/IEC 27001

End-to-end readiness and remediation for the international standard for information security management systems.

  • Risk assessment & treatment plans
  • Access control matrices & IAM hardening
  • Asset inventory and data classification
  • Backup, DR and business continuity playbooks
  • Cryptographic controls (KMS, TLS, key rotation)
PASSED

SOC 2 (Type I & II)

Designing technical controls that satisfy the AICPA Trust Services Criteria — and producing the evidence to prove it.

  • Centralized audit logging (CloudTrail, GuardDuty, Security Hub)
  • Change management via Git + IaC review gates
  • Vendor & third-party access reviews
  • Encryption at rest & in transit, secret rotation
  • Continuous monitoring & alerting on control violations
BUILT

Greenfield Infrastructure

Building net-new infrastructure with security and compliance baked in from day one — instead of bolting them on later.

  • Multi-account landing zones with Control Tower
  • Network segmentation & private connectivity by default
  • Hardened Kubernetes baselines (CIS, OPA/Gatekeeper)
  • Pre-wired observability and incident workflows
  • Documented runbooks and tested DR scenarios
